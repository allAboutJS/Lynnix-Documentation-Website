Why Lynnix

Structure for htmx apps without the framework overhead.

htmx brings back the simplicity of server-rendered HTML. Lynnix gives it the routing and rendering layer it deserves — file-based conventions, automatic partial responses, and error boundaries, all built on Mutor.js.
